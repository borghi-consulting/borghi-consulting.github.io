02 / 09
We've been exactly where you are.
AB
Alessandro Borghi
Founder · Borghi Consulting™

Alessandro had spent years operating at the highest levels of business strategy, consulting, development, entrepreneurship. He entered crypto the way most people do — through a trusted friend. A platform, a promise, a narrative that sounded solid. He put money in and lost everything.

Trying to recover, he did what anyone would: he tried again. Another platform, more people around him doing the same thing. He lost again.

Then came the gurus, the influencers, the narratives. He didn't just follow them — he believed them. And ended up with a portfolio full of tokens worth next to nothing, high exposure, and still no real direction. Every attempt to fix it led somewhere that looked like progress. And wasn't.

What struck him wasn't just the loss. It was the absence of any real framework in a space that was moving billions of dollars and leaving people completely exposed. Nobody was building the structure this space desperately needed.

That became the problem he decided to solve.

03 / 09
We've been exactly where you are.
GD
Giulia D'Agostino
Co-Founder · Borghi Consulting™

Giulia came from a different world entirely. She was a digital creative, and for years she had watched her work disappear into platforms that profited from it while the people who made it saw almost nothing.

When NFTs emerged, it felt like an answer — not an investment, but a structural shift. A space where creators could finally own what they built and where the value of the work could return to the person who made it. She didn't come in chasing returns. She came in because she believed the idea was right.

For a while, it was. Then it collapsed. The tokens became worthless, the rewards disappeared, and the community that had felt real scattered without warning. The money, the idea, the people — gone at once.

What remained was a very specific understanding. She had seen how a space can feel open and transparent while being built to obscure — how the language that welcomes people in is the same language that ensures they never fully understand what they are walking into.

That became the foundation of everything she built next.

04 / 09
The question that changed everything.

After the losses. After the rebuilding. After the gurus, the influencers, the portfolios that never quite worked — they stopped. And asked a different question.

Not "how do we recover?" Not "which asset performs next?" — What does someone in this space actually need — not the expert, but the person who is genuinely trying to understand it?

That question changed everything.

Not a pivot. Not a rebrand. A decision to take everything that had gone wrong, study it seriously, understand it structurally, and build something that actually served the people the industry was leaving behind.

What followed was thousands of hours of study, courses, seminars, deep research into blockchain mechanics, asset allocation, risk management, on-chain analysis, DeFi structure, fraud and scam recognition.

The professional foundation was already there. What was missing in the entire space was the rigorous knowledge it demands and almost never provides.

So they built it.
