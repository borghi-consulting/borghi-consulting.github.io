ABOUT BORGHI CONSULTING™

Most people in crypto don't lack information. They lack structure.

They're holding assets they don't fully understand. Making decisions based on noise, not conviction. Following voices that disappear the moment markets turn.

No framework. No plan. No one actually in their corner.

That's the problem Borghi Consulting™ was built to solve.

And to understand why — you need to know how we got here.

ALESSANDRO

Alessandro had spent years operating at the highest levels of business strategy, consulting, development, entrepreneurship. He entered crypto the way most people do — through a trusted friend. A platform, a promise, a narrative that sounded solid. He put money in and lost everything.

Trying to recover, he did what anyone would: he tried again. Another platform, more people around him doing the same thing. He lost again.

Then came the gurus, the influencers, the narratives. He didn't just follow them — he believed them. And ended up with a portfolio full of tokens worth next to nothing, high exposure, and still no real direction.

"What struck him wasn't just the loss. It was the absence of any real framework in a space that was moving billions of dollars and leaving people completely exposed. Nobody was building the structure this space desperately needed."

GIULIA

Giulia came from a different world entirely. She was a digital creative, and for years she had watched her work disappear into platforms that profited from it while the people who made it saw almost nothing.

When NFTs emerged, it felt like an answer — not an investment, but a structural shift. A space where creators could finally own what they built and where the value of the work could return to the person who made it.

She didn't come in chasing returns. She came in because she believed the idea was right. For a while, it was. Then it collapsed. The tokens became worthless, the rewards disappeared, and the community that had felt real scattered without warning.

"She had seen how a space can feel open and transparent while being built to obscure — how the language that welcomes people in is the same language that ensures they never fully understand what they are walking into."
