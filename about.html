Our story

About us

Structure before speculation. Most people in crypto don't lack information. They lack structure. That's the problem Borghi Consulting™ was built to solve.

We've been exactly where you are.

Alessandro entered the crypto space the way most people do — through a trusted friend. A platform, a promise, a narrative that sounded solid. He put money in. He lost everything. Then, trying to recover, he did what anyone would do: he tried again. Another platform. Someone mentioned Bitcoin. More people around him doing the same thing. He lost again.

After that, he did what the space encouraged — he followed the gurus. Watched the influencers. Chased the narratives. And ended up with a portfolio full of tokens worth next to nothing, high exposure, and still no real direction. Every attempt to fix it led somewhere that looked like progress and wasn't.

Coming from a senior management and C-level consulting background, the blow wasn't just financial. It was personal. Someone who had built a career on structured thinking and sound decision-making had been undone by a space with no real framework to stand on.

Giulia's entry point was different. She came in through creativity — NFTs, a platform that rewarded minting, a world that seemed to sit at the intersection of art and technology. For a couple of months, everything worked. Then it collapsed. The tokens, the rewards, the platform — gone. What remained was something specific: a deep understanding of how poor communication and deliberate obscurity create vulnerability. How jargon isn't just confusing, it's a weapon. And how the people most likely to get hurt are often the ones most genuinely trying to engage.

The question that changed everything

After the losses. After the rebuilding. After the gurus and the influencers and the portfolios that never quite worked — we stopped. And we asked a different question.

What does someone in this space actually need — not the expert, but the person who is genuinely trying to understand it?

Not a pivot. Not a rebrand. A decision to take everything that had gone wrong, study it seriously, understand it structurally, and build something that actually served the people the industry was leaving behind.

What followed was thousands of hours of study. Courses, seminars, deep research into blockchain mechanics, asset allocation, risk management, on-chain analysis, DeFi structure, fraud and scam recognition. The professional foundation was already there — senior consulting, strategic management, communication direction. What was added was the specific, rigorous knowledge that the crypto space demands and rarely provides.
